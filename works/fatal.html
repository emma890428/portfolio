The Influential Factors of Traffic Fatality Rates

This information visualization web page helps viewers to investigate the correlation relationship between traffic fatality rate and alcohol consumption or vehicle quantity.

The heatmap demonstrates the difference of traffic fatality rates in state level with color coded. The line and bar chart below displays either alcohol consumption or vehicle quantity for each state by the bars, according to viewersâ€™ selection. And traffic fatality rate in shown by a line in this chart. It allows viewer to visualize the comparison between the two variables of all the states to figure out whether there is a correlation. By scrolling on the timeline on the top of the page, the viewer can get the data of a specific year in both two graphs. The line chart on the right displays all detailed data of selected state overtime from 2004 to 2009.

This application also provides nice interaction elements such as a timeline scrollbar, a checkbox for sorting animation and a list for selecting a specific state, which make users understand the data easily.
