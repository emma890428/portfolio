Hometown Brochure
- Beijing

Beijing is my hometown which I miss so much. I miss every building, culture and food there.

Beijing is not only an ancient, tranditional city, but also a modern metropolis. This brochure introduces Beijing from both ancient and modern aspects, using beautiful photographs including famouse buildings, places of interest, culture and food. It presents the diversity of Beijing. The pictures of ancient places of interest such as the Great Wall show the long history. And the photo of the modern buildings in Central Business District demonstrates the prosperity of new Beijing.

The design uses bright compound colors to make viewers feel fresh and energetic. The Sans-serif typography makes the text clean and easy to read.
